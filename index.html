St. George’s College, Aruvithura, Kottayam, Kerala, India, is proud to host the International Conference on Recent Advancements in Science, Social Science, and Technology (ICRASST) 2024, organized by the IQAC on December 3 and 4, 2024. This two-day hybrid event offers a platform for knowledge exchange through invited talks by eminent personalities, oral and poster presentations, and networking opportunities. Participants from all disciplines can present their research either online or offline, with awards for the best presentations. Selected full papers will be published in GEM journal, and all abstracts will be published with an ISBN. We invite you to join us in this enriching academic experience.

Theme

The International Conference on Recent Advancements in Science, Social Science, and Technology (ICRASST) 2024 will focus on pivotal themes, including Advancements in Science and Technology, Innovations in Commerce and Management, Interdisciplinary Approaches to Sustainable Development, Emerging Trends in Food Science and Technology, and Mathematical Modeling and Computational Techniques. The conference will also feature specialized tracks in Physics and Materials Science, Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, Botany and Environmental Science, Mathematics and Statistics, Computer Applications and IT, Food Science and Technology, Commerce and Management, and Innovation and Entrepreneurship, encouraging cross-disciplinary collaboration and exploration.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, scholars, and professionals to submit their original research for presentation at the International Conference on Recent Advancements in Science, Social Science, and Technology (ICRASST) 2024. Submissions are welcome across a wide range of themes, including science and technology, commerce and management, sustainable development, food science, and mathematical modeling. Selected papers will have the opportunity to be published in peer-reviewed journals or as book chapters. Join us in sharing your insights and contributing to the advancement of knowledge. For submission guidelines and more details, please visit our Call for Papers page.

Key Dates

Submission deadline for research papers October 18, 2024
Acceptance notification November 8, 2024
Final Camera-ready paper submission November 22, 2024
